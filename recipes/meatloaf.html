Meatloaf

Description

This is one of the most simple meatloaf recipes. It's one of the first recipes my mom taught me as a little tike. I'm happy to share this classic American home staple for you to try as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/8 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1.5 ounce packet of meatloaf seasoning
  • 1/4 cup of ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, oats, egg, salt, pepper and meatloaf seasoning. Mix until there is an even spread of ingredients throughout the meat.
  3. Form the meat into a loaf and place on a baking sheet.
  4. Top the loaf with ketchup and bake uncovered for about 40 minutes or until bubbly.
