Beef Taco Skillet

Description

This recipe brings back a lot of memories of my dad cooking. His specialty was always taco pie. What better way to similate that flavor and nostalgia than taco pie in it's simplest form? Check out this awesome recipe for a quick and delicious Mexican-American dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of salsa
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar, mozerella, or mexican blend
  • 6 flour tortillas cut into 1" pieces
  • Salt, pepper, lime and sour cream for taste
  • Additionally you can add a can of black beans or corn (drained) for added flavors

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add the remaining wet ingredients.
  3. Add cheese and cover until melted.
  4. Add tortillas and warm.
