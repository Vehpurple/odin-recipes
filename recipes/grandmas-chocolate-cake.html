Grandma's Chocolate Cake

Description

This is the best chocolate cake I've ever had, hands down! My grandma handed this recipe down to my mother who passed it down to me. I'm pretty sure she just got the recipe from the back of a Hershey'sTM cocoa box. It's sure to become your favorite as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of baking cocoa
  • A dash of salt
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix together the dry ingredients.
  3. Blend together: 1 cup of boiling water and baking soda.
  4. Add water mix to the dry ingredients.
  5. Add in a 1/2 stick of melted butter, 1 egg and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth.
  6. Grease and flour a 9"x9" (or equivalent) cake pan.
  7. Bake for approximately 25-35 minutes or until done.
