No Bake Cookies

Description

Here's another great classic recipe. This dessert is so easy to make and delicious to eat, so let's just jump right in!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of dry or powdered milk
  • 1/2 cupp of water
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa
  • 1/2 cup of peanut butter
  • 3 cups of instant rolled oats
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla

Steps

  1. Mix together and bring to a boil: sugar, butter, dry milk, and water.
  2. Once boiling, remove mixture from heat and add in: cocoa, peanut butter, oats and vanilla.
  3. Spoon by the teaspoon onto a wax paper covered cookie sheet.
